About Us

Treble Solutions, led by a single Black woman, proudly holds a level 1 BEE (Broad-Based Black Economic Empowerment) status in the Telco space and has been in operation since 2020.

Our core success is rooted in the firm belief that human interaction remains essential in today's digital age, with businesses increasingly valuing this personalized support. We recognize the artistry required in handling client interactions over the phone, alongside the scientific precision of modern technology for information management. Our solutions seamlessly blend these elements to maximize benefits for our clients.
We dedicate our intelligence to crafting seamless and transparent call handling resolutions, with our people serving as our greatest asset. Through their training and unwavering commitment, we deliver exceptional service to all our clients. Committed to long-term partnerships, we take pride in the expertise and specialized support we offer.
Moreover, we are proud to affirm that we are fully FAIS (Financial Advisory and Intermediary Services) and NCR (National Credit Regulator) compliant, further underscoring our commitment to regulatory standards and best practices.
